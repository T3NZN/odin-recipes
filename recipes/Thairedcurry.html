Thai Red Curry

Description

This is a quick and easy Thai curry made with either chicken, shrimp, or any meat of your liking cut into bite size pieces(I'll be using shrimp as an example).

You can even use tofu to make it vegetarian. Coconut milk and red thai curry paste make an irrestible sauce. It gets ready real fast with very little prep required.

Ingredients

(yields 4 servings)

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 lbs shrimp(do not cut shrimp, just devein and remove shell but leave tail on)
  • 1tbsp thai red curry paste
  • 1 can(13.66 oz) coconut milk(either fat free or regular)
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock(or vegetable)
  • 1tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 3 cups assorted cut-up vegetables, such as bell peppers, carrots, onions and sugar snap peas
  • 2 tbsp basil julienned(preferrably thai basil)
  • cooked jasmine rice to serve with(optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in large skillet on medium heat. add curry paste, stir fry until fragrant. Add coconut milk and chicken stock, bring to simmer on medium-high heat. Stir in brown sugar and fish sauce until well blended.
  2. Stir in meat and vegetables, simmer until meat is cooked through and vegetables are tender-crisp. Remove from heat and stir in basil.
  3. Serve over cooked jasmine rice, if you prefer.
