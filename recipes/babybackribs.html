Baby Back Ribs

Description

These are tender and the meat falls right off the bone.

Generally, people think of baby back ribs as meals they would only order when at a restaurant, but they are so easy to make at home. This recipe could not be any more simple.

Ingredients

yields 4 servings

  • 2 lbs pork baby back ribs
  • 1(18oz) bottle of your favorite barbecue sauce

Steps

  1. Tear off 4 pieces of aluminum foil big enough to enclose each portion of ribs. Spray each foil with vegetable cooking spray. Brush the ribs liberally with barbecue sauce and place each portion in its own piece of foil. Wrap tightly and refrigerate for at least 8 hrs, or overnight
  2. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F(150 degrees C).
  3. Bake ribs wrapped tightly in the foilat 300 degrees F for 2 1/2 hours. Remove from foil and add more sauce if desired.
